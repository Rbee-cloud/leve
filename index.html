Meet Aditya

\

\ Hi! I'm Aditya, the founder of Leve. Our mission is simple: to help young people, like you and me, build resilience and learn how to deal with life's ups and downs.\

\

\ It all started in a small town in Portugal, where I realized that many of us need a safe space to talk, share, and grow together. At Leve, we believe you don't have to carry the weight of the world on your own\'97it's so much easier when you've got a community by your side.\

\

\ We want to create moments to connect, activities that make us feel good, and, above all, show that it's totally okay to ask for help. Being strong doesn't mean having no problems\'97it means having the courage to face them, step by step.\
